Re-cycling ♻

As part of our company's mission, we offer our clients a recycling programme. The environment is our home, thus we have a responsibility to take care of it.

We take back worn out and damaged jerry cans, bottles and other plastics in an effort to keep the environment plastic free. The recovered plastic is repurposed at our plant. The badly damaged ones are turned into industry recognized palnto bricks used in the construction and landscaping business.
